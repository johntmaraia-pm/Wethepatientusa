About Me

My Background

I am a Registered Nurse, BSN, RN. I have over 30 years of nursing experience working in various fields including Med-Surg, Cardiovascular, Oncology, Gastroenterology, Wound Care, and Registered Nurse Advocacy. During the past few years, I have done daily research on Covid.

My Goals

As a Registered Nurse Advocate, my primary goal is to help patients navigate through the healthcare system. I will communicate to both patients and families, giving them peace of mind knowing they are being taken care of.

Things to Know

When you choose me as your Registered Nurse Advocate, you now have a voice that will be heard by doctors and healthcare providers. It takes courage and hands-on experience to work together and at times challenge the healthcare system.
